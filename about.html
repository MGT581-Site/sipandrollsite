About Us

Sip & Roll was founded by Maria Barrios and Hailey Howell with a mission to bring joy, creativity, and connection to every celebration. We believe in community, sustainability, and making people feel special. With a retro Airstream turned bar and a team of certified bartenders, we’re here to serve more than just drinks—we serve experiences.

We’re proud to be locally owned and rooted in community-driven values. We give back, hire local, and create memories one pour at a time.
