Services

Full-Service Mobile Bar: Our vintage Airstream arrives stocked with bar tools, cups, coolers, ice bins, and custom decor. Includes two professional bartenders for up to 3 hours of service, custom cocktail menu consultation, mocktail options, and cleanup service.

Bartender-Only Package: Two licensed bartenders arrive with all the tools, mixers, and setup elements required. Ideal for locations with an existing bar setup.

Customization & Add-ons:

  • Lighting and sound system: $100 - $250
  • Custom signage or branded drinkware: starting at $75
  • Floral & rustic decor: starting at $150

Booking Info:

  • Full-service: Starts at $1,200 for 3 hours, plus $300/hour after
  • Bartender-only: $30/hour per bartender (2 hour minimum)

Note: Alcohol must be purchased by the client. Sip & Roll works with licensed vendors to assist with planning and ordering for best pricing and legal compliance in Michigan.
